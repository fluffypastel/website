We're the community!

Foaling Around is a mainly closed space for the MLP:FiM and beyond foal fans! The community is maintained by Fluffy Pastel and her friends.

Why foals?

Because they're fun!

Fillies and colts are often underrated favorite characters around the mares and stallions, we simply want to give them more attention.

Community?

Indeed!

The little/big community, we're not only just sharing and create cute arts but make our own projects, gaming events and occasionally support for new-bees.

Projects?
